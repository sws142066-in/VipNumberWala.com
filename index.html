VIP Mobile Numbers — General

What is a VIP mobile number?
A VIP mobile number is a sequence of digits with an easy-to-recall, memorable pattern (e.g., 9999000000, 1234567890). They are valued for status, branding, and numerology — essentially giving your contact details a premium upgrade.
How to buy a fancy mobile number online in India?
Visit VIP Number Wala, search your preferred pattern, choose a number from our exclusive inventory, complete the secure payment, and we will immediately send you the Unique Porting Code (UPC). The process is quick, legal, and hassle-free.
Is buying a VIP number legal in India?
Yes, it is 100% legal. VIP Number Wala provides a valid Unique Porting Code (UPC) for all sales. This code allows you to port the number to any network (Jio, Airtel, Vi, BSNL) under your name, fully compliant with TRAI regulations.
What is the price of a VVIP mobile number?
Prices for VVIP mobile numbers vary widely, from a few thousand rupees for simple fancy patterns to over ₹1,00,000 for ultra-rare sequences (like Quadruple Zeros or pure sequential numbers). Prices are clearly displayed on our listings.
How long does it take to activate a fancy mobile number?
After you submit the UPC to your chosen operator, the Mobile Number Portability (MNP) process typically takes 3 to 7 business days for activation. Patience — VIP status is worth the wait!

Do you sell 786 mobile numbers?

What is a mirror mobile number?
A mirror mobile number is a palindrome — where the number reads the same forwards and backward (e.g., 9898989898). We have a dedicated category for these unique numbers — they're truly easy to remember.
Can I get a mobile number matching my date of birth?
Yes, our advanced search tool allows you to input your date, year, or anniversary date to find numbers that contain that specific digit sequence, ensuring your number is truly personal.

UPC & Porting Process

What is UPC (Unique Porting Code)?
The UPC is an 8-digit alpha-numeric code required for MNP. Once your purchase is confirmed, we deliver the UPC code to you via SMS and email within 24 hours. You use this to claim ownership from the telecom operator.
What if the UPC expires or the number is invalid?
VIP Number Wala offers a 100% Money Back Guarantee. If you encounter any issue with the UPC or number validation during the porting process, we guarantee a full and fast refund.
Can I port this fancy number to any network?
Yes. The number is legally transferable and compatible with all major Indian telecom operators (Airtel, Jio, Vi, BSNL). You can port it to prepaid or postpaid as per your choice.
Do I have to visit the store after buying the number?
Yes, the only offline step is visiting the nearest retail outlet of your chosen operator (Jio/Airtel/Vi) with your ID proof and the UPC to initiate the MNP process and complete e-KYC. We handle the rest.
Can I choose a new number starting with a specific digit series?
Yes, our 'Start With' search filter allows you to select your preferred first few digits to find a unique, easy-to-remember series that fits your requirement perfectly.
